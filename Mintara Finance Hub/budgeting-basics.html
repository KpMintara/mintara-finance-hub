Why Budgeting Matters

  • Clarity: See your income and expenses at a glance.
  • Control: Decide your priorities instead of reacting to bills.
  • Achieve Goals: Save for emergencies, travel, education, or retirement.
  • Reduce Stress: Eliminate money worries by staying on track.

5 Steps to Your First Budget

  1. Calculate Net Income: Add up all take-home pay (after taxes).
  2. List Expenses: Record fixed costs (rent, utilities) and variable costs (groceries, entertainment).
  3. Set Spending Categories: Allocate dollars to essentials, savings, debt repayment, and fun.
  4. Choose a Method:
    • 50/30/20 Rule: 50% needs, 30% wants, 20% savings/debt.
    • Zero-Based: Every dollar has a job‚Äîincome minus expenses equals zero.
    • Envelope System: Cash in envelopes for each category.
  5. Track & Adjust: Review weekly and tweak categories as life changes.
